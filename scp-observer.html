ABOUT SCP: OBSERVER

A containment breach has unleashed several SCP entities, leaving you—a maintenance technician—trapped in a solitary "safe room." Your routine task of reviewing electronic devices has suddenly become a desperate struggle for survival.

As chaos unfolds around you, your mission is clear: manage dwindling power supplies and utilize surveillance equipment to keep the horrifying entities at bay. Every decision is critical as you work to maintain the integrity of your sanctuary until help arrives.

Drawing inspiration from the atmospheric tension of Five Nights at Freddy's and the expansive lore of SCP - Containment Breach, SCP: Observer plunges you into a heart-pounding race against time. Your skills, wit, and nerve will be tested to their limits as you face the terror that lurks just beyond your walls.

Will you endure long enough for rescue to reach you?
